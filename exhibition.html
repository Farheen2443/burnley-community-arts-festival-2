Exhibition Artists

Clair Smith

Clair Smith is a contemporary mixed-media artist inspired by natural landscapes and soft textures. Her work explores calmness, nostalgia, and emotional storytelling through layered colour and form.

Sophie Randall

Sophie Randall is an illustrator known for warm colours, expressive lines, and community-focused themes. Her art highlights personal moments and the beauty of everyday connections.

Daniel Whitmore

Daniel Whitmore creates digital abstract artwork using geometric forms and vivid colour theory. His pieces explore structure, movement, and the blend of technology with creativity.

Rohan Sharma

Rohan Sharma mixes traditional ink styles with modern experimental techniques. His work reflects identity, heritage, and the merging of symbolic motifs with expressive patterns.
